About Wheng's

A small kitchen, a big love for traditional kakanin.

Wheng's Food Products Manufacturing began in 2020 with a simple promise — to bring the taste of home-cooked Filipino rice cakes back to the table. From Puto Maya and Suman to Bibingka, Biko, and Chocolate Moron, each recipe is made the way lola taught: slowly, patiently, and always from scratch.

We use 100% pure malagkit rice and freshly squeezed coconut cream — all natural, nothing artificial. Every order is freshly made so you get that just-wrapped warmth with every bite.

Organic ingredients
Pure malagkit rice, freshly squeezed coconut cream, and natural sweeteners — never shortcuts.
Handmade daily
Every piece is wrapped, steamed, and finished by hand in our family kitchen.
No preservatives
We make to order so it reaches you as it should be — soft, fragrant, and fresh.
